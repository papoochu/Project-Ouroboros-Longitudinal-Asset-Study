# Project-Ouroboros-Longitudinal-Asset-Study
**PROJECT OUROBOROS: LONGITUDINAL ASSET STUDY** is an ongoing classified initiative under the authority of the fictional Consultative Assembly of Eden, dedicated to the systematic documentation, evaluation, and longitudinal tracking of all associated operatives and assets.

This archive consolidates field reports, psychological assessments, and organizational correspondences pertaining to each Council member, with periodic amendments reflecting newly recovered or declassified material. Entries are presented in accordance with AE documentation protocol and remain subject to redaction and internal review.

Notice: Data integrity is provisional and under continuous revision as further intelligence becomes available.
