A precision instrument; handle with calibrated empathy and firm control protocols.

II. EXECUTIVE SYNOPSIS

Asset is a high-value, low-volatility operative defined by a pathological drive for order and a profound rejection of human fallibility. Her utility is unparalleled in systems architecture and long-term strategic planning. Core vulnerability is a blind spot for chaotic, unpredictable human variables, which she attempts to solve with increasing layers of control. Her allegiance is to the Council's stated mission of global stability, which she interprets as the imposition of a perfect, machine-like order. A strict, samurai-derived honor code governs her actions; she requires a logical, defensible reason for all violence, making her predictable but occasionally obstructive to more expedient methods. She is a foundational pillar of the Assembly's operational infrastructure; her continued functionality is paramount. Handle with respect for her capabilities and a clear, logical rationale for all requests.

III. BACKGROUND

  • During her transition to cybernetic perception, DE LA SANGRE personally retaught her swordsmanship, translating her traditional forms into a new, hyper-efficient style based on auditory and data-stream cues.
